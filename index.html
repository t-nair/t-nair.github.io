Advancing Accessibility for Engineering Education

Institution: University of Washington Seattle

Mentors: Prof. Anant P. Anantram, Dr. Lalitha Subramanian

Summary: Developed an automated pipeline using n8n, Python, and Azure OCR to convert PowerPoint presentations into accessible formats for blind/low-vision students. The system extracts text, generates alt text for images, and produces screen-reader-friendly documents at scale, improving accessibility in higher education STEM courses.

  • Automated extraction and conversion of slide content
  • Integration with cloud OCR and AI image captioning
  • Batch processing for large course content
